Privacy & Data
Policy

Your data never leaves your device. Period.

ToolVerse is engineered with a "Privacy-by-Design" philosophy. We use modern browser technology to process files locally, ensuring you never have to trust a server with your private documents.

1. Local Processing Engine

All utilities on ToolVerse—including PDF mergers, image compressors, and AI text generators—run within your browser's isolated sandbox. We utilize WebAssembly (WASM) to perform heavy calculations locally. We do not transmit your file data to our servers.

2. Information Collection

ToolVerse is an anonymous platform. We do not collect:

  • Personal Identity (Names, Emails, Addresses)
  • File Metadata or Contents
  • User-Generated Prompt History

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use standard analytics tools (GA4) to understand traffic patterns and prioritize which tools to improve. These tools collect anonymized data such as device type and country. We also use browser LocalStorage to save your favorite tools for your next visit.

4. Third-Party Advertising

Our platform is free because we display ads. Partners like Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this or other websites. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your Google settings.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this policy, please reach out to:
